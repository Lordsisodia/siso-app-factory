# Templates

Vertical starter kits that compose platform packages + (soon) experience packs.

- `bike-rental/` – extracts booking, catalog, admin, and review logic from Five Star Car Hire.
- `tour-guides/` – includes booking APIs, notifications, QR tickets, media management from Mallorca Activities.

Over time, new verticals (restaurants, hospitality, commerce) will move here instead of `packages/*`.
